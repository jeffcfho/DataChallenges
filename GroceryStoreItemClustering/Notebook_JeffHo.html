Analysis¶

  1. Project items into a lower dimensionality space (e.g., not the vector of ~24000 users for each item)
  2. Cluster the item vectors together user K-means
  3. Identify clusters of items and make recommendations based on clusters.

def pct_carts_in(item_id1,item_id2):
    """For any two items, return percentage of total carts they were in """
